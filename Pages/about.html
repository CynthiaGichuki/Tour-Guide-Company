ABOUT US

We are Africa Group Safari Tours, a team of experienced and passionate tour guides who specialize in providing unforgettable safari experiences across Africa. Our company was founded with a vision to share the natural beauty and rich culture of Africa with the rest of the world.

Our Team

Our team consists of local guides who have a deep knowledge of the destinations we offer,
and who are committed to providing personalized and authentic experiences to our clients.
We are dedicated to ensuring that our guests have a safe, comfortable, and memorable journey through Africa.

Our Mission

At Africa Group Safari Tours, our mission is to create unforgettable safari experiences
that showcase the natural beauty and rich culture of Africa,
while promoting responsible tourism practices and supporting local communities.
