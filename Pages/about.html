About Us

We are Africa Group Safari Tours, a team of experienced and passionate tour guides who specialize in providing unforgettable safari experiences across Africa. Our company was founded with a vision to share the natural beauty and rich culture of Africa with the rest of the world.

Our team consists of local guides who have a deep knowledge of the destinations we offer, and who are committed to providing personalized and authentic experiences to our clients. We are dedicated to ensuring that our guests have a safe, comfortable, and memorable journey through Africa.

Our Mission

At Africa Group Safari Tours, our mission is to create unforgettable safari experiences that showcase the natural beauty and rich culture of Africa, while promoting responsible tourism practices and supporting local communities.

Our Values

  • Professionalism: We uphold the highest standards of professionalism in everything we do, from our interactions with clients to our business operations.
  • Responsibility: We are committed to promoting responsible tourism practices that protect the environment and support local communities.
  • Passion: We are passionate about sharing the beauty and culture of Africa with the world, and strive to create experiences that inspire our clients.
  • Personalization: We believe that every traveler is unique, and we work closely with our clients to create customized itineraries that meet their individual needs and preferences.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring that our clients have a memorable and enjoyable safari experience.
